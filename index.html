Welcome to the Physics of driven and active matter webpage!

Our research lies broadly in the domain of nonequilibrium phenomena, with a particular interest in collective behavior, optimal control, and dynamical phase transitions. Through theoretical and computational approaches, we study how complex many-body systems organize, fluctuate, and respond when driven away from equilibrium.
