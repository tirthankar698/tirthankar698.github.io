Welcome

Welcome to my research webpage. My research lies broadly in the statistical physics of driven and active matter, with a particular interest in nonequilibrium phenomena, collective behavior, optimal control, and biologically inspired active systems. Through theoretical and computational approaches, I study how complex many-body systems organize, fluctuate, and respond when driven away from equilibrium.
